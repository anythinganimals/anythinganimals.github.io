Anything Animals is a program that brings the zoo to you. We are an educational program that teaches all about the wild world of animals, although we specialize in reptiles, our program also includes both mammals and avians. Anything Animals is specialized in giving our audience a unique, hands on up close and educational experience with exotic animals.

Located in Marietta GA. Animal shows are usually between 30-45 minutes. You can however request how long the presentation will be, and which animals to make sure to cover if this is an educational program rather than for a birthday party. Price is based on three things, how far away, how long the show is via personal request and on where we will be performing, be it a Birthday party, school, scout event, library, block party, church or other. A few things to consider before contacting/booking a show: how large the group or audience is, the location of a private residence, the event taking place indoors or outdoors, and if there are any special instructions regarding where to park or unload.
